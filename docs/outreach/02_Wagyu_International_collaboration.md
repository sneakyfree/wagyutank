Dear Mr. Bennett,

I'm Grant Whitmer, founder of WagyuTank.com. Before anything else: Wagyu
International is the finest independent resource on the breed anywhere — your
foundation histories and country-by-country accounts have been an education for us,

I'm writing because our missions overlap. WagyuTank is building a knowledge hub and

Rather than duplicate your life's work, I'd love to explore whether there's a way to
**collaborate and cross-reference** — for example, WagyuTank citing and linking to
your foundation and country pages as the authoritative source, and (if you're ever
open to it) co-developing or fact-checking foundation-animal content together. Your
name carries weight in this breed that ours never will, and we'd rather build on your
shoulders, with your blessing and attribution, than reinvent it.

If any of this is of interest — even just an introduction and a conversation — I'd be
glad for it. Thank you for the encyclopedia; it's a gift to the whole community.

With genuine respect,
